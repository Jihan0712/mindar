How brand accounts are created

How to get a brand account

  1. Contact the INRL administrator
  2. Provide your brand name and login email
  3. The admin creates your account in the dashboard
  4. You receive login credentials to use below

Already have credentials?

Sign in with the email and password provided by the admin. You'll be taken to the brand AR dashboard.

Sign in to your brand account →

What brand accounts can do

  • ✓ Upload and manage AR targets
  • ✓ Link products to AR experiences
  • ✓ Access the brand AR dashboard
  • ✗ Cannot access other brands' data

Self-registration for brand accounts is disabled for security reasons.
Contact contact@inrl.com to request access.
